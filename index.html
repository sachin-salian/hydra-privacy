💧 Hydra – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Hydra ("the App") is a personal hydration tracking app built to help you stay healthy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Storage

All data you enter in Hydra — including water intake logs, daily goals, streaks, and settings — is stored entirely on your device using local device storage (AsyncStorage). No data is ever sent to or stored on any external server.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Personal information (name, email, location)
  • Health or fitness data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

3. Notifications

If you enable hydration reminders, the App uses your device's local notification system to schedule alerts. These notifications are generated on-device only — no notification data is sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
